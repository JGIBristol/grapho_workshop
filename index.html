Visualising your Visualisations workshop¶

The Workshop¶

This is a 1-hour workshop to support research into the decisions we make when we visualise data.

In the workshop, we will:

  • Tell you about the What Aren’t You Seeing? (WAYS) research project.

  • Help you to get up and running with the Grapho package, which will record code and image histories during a data visualisation challenge.

  • Give you a short data visualisation challenge

  • Show you how to visualise your visualisations
